Mobirise

 One comfortably warm night, I dreamed about holding a Canon DSLR Camera. No hints where that idea came from. I don't even have an idea how to use a DSLR. The following morning, I immediately got up to fetch my father at the airport and was surprised of the Canon Bag hanging in his shoulders. This is where our story begins.


         Hi! We are Team Thumbmark- a Bohol-based Wedding Photographers, a group of young individuals who has love for Photography and had different ways in expressing photos with Creativity. Thus then was able to create a perfect team. Micah Lumaad and Salve Regina Mendros, are a Bohol-based Wedding Photographers and Founder of Thumbmark Photography. Micah and I started as best of friends. (*FRIEND-ZONED Level 9999). Micah had the passion for Photography and I have a camera. (*see?! We're meant to be best friends). There are plenty of events in our life that we see a very heart-melting moment, hearing a very emotional love song and at the back of our minds we keep saying "How I wish I can capture this and make a story out of it.". We then decided to engage ourselves into Wedding Photography being inspired by the works of Mr. and Mrs. Toto Villaruel and Nicolai Melicor-Villaruel (A Manila-based Wedding Photographer). It has always been a very wonderful opportunity to be able to capture such wonderful moments, seeing people laugh, the warm embraces of two different families being bonded with love and the tears that fall as the bride walks the aisle. These are moments that we cannot dare to miss, moments that we know that no one could ever bring back. 


The main goal of the team is to "Capture Moments". And we are hoping that the next awesome moments that we'll capture would be yours.
